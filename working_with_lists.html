Jollof Rice Recipe


Ingredients

  • Blended pepper
  • Onions
  • Tomato paste
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • Sweet corn or other vegetables
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Put the washed pot on the gas stove and wait for it to dry
  2. Add groundnut oil to the dried pot
  3. Add tomato paste
  4. Let it fry before adding blended pepper
  5. Then add part of the maggi and salt
  6. Add water
  7. Add spices and cover to let the broth boil
  8. Add your rice and wait for the broth to dry
